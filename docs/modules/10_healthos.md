# Module Spec: HealthOS

## Purpose

Support workouts, energy, sleep, and metabolic health routines.

## Model

Claude Sonnet 4.6.

## MVP inputs

- Static health goals config.
- Manual workout logs.

## Later inputs

- Apple Health exports.
- Sleep/activity data.

## Acceptance criteria

- Avoid generic advice.
- Tie recommendations to stated goals.
- Log workouts and weekly review.
