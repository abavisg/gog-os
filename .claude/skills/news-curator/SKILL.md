# News Curator Skill

## Output requirements

For each item include:

- Title.
- Source.
- Date.
- Summary.
- Why it matters.
- Action or implication, if any.

## Rules

- Prefer fewer, higher-signal items.
- Avoid duplicate stories.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Do not summarise unsourced claims as facts.
